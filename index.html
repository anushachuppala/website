About Us

Rolls-Royce is a British company known for manufacturing both luxury automobiles and industrial power systems like aircraft engines, marine propulsion, and power systems. The luxury car division is a subsidiary of BMW and is renowned for its handcrafted, highly customizable vehicles like the Phantom and Cullinan. The industrial division, Rolls-Royce plc, is a separate entity that designs and builds power systems for civil aerospace, defense, and power sectors. Rolls-Royce Motor Cars: Identity: A luxury car manufacturer, wholly owned by BMW AG, that builds exclusive and opulent vehicles. Philosophy: Focuses on artistry, high-end craftsmanship, and personalization through its Bespoke program, where cars are hand-made to order. Key Models: Includes models such as the Phantom, Ghost, and Cullinan SUV, with special editions like the Spectre electric vehicle.
